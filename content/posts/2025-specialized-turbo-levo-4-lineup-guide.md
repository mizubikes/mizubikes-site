## The 2025 Specialized Turbo Levo 4: Power, Range, and Ride Quality

The 2025 **Specialized Turbo Levo 4** lineup represents the next evolution in full-power electric mountain bikes. With redesigned geometry, advanced electronics, and suspension tech like **Genie shock** across the board, every Levo 4 model is built to deliver high performance and confidence across all types of trail terrain.

There are four models in the current Levo 4 family:

- S-Works Turbo Levo 4
- Turbo Levo 4 Pro
- Turbo Levo 4 Expert
- Turbo Levo 4 Comp

All models share the same FACT 11m carbon frame with adjustable geometry and SWAT internal storage, 160mm of front and rear travel, and Specialized's custom-tuned Genie shock platform. However, key differences exist in **motor power**, **drivetrain components**, and **suspension spec**.

* * *

## Motor & Battery: Power Where It Counts

### S-Works Turbo Levo 4

- **Motor**: Specialized 3.1 S-Works
- **Torque**: 111 Nm
- **Max Power**: 720W
- **Battery**: 840Wh

### Pro, Expert & Comp Models

- **Motor**: Specialized 3.1
- **Torque**: 101 Nm
- **Max Power**: 666W
- **Battery**: 840Wh

While all models offer impressive range and support, the **S-Works** version steps it up with 15% more torque and higher peak wattage for longer climbs, stronger acceleration, and an overall more aggressive ride feel.

* * *

## Suspension: Genie Shock Across the Lineup

All Levo 4 models feature **FOX rear shocks with Specialized's Genie Technology**, improving mid-stroke support and small bump sensitivity across rough and technical terrain.

Front suspension differs:

- **S-Works & Pro**: FOX 38 Factory GRIPX2
- **Expert**: FOX 38 Performance Elite GRIPX2
- **Comp**: FOX 36 Rhythm GRIP

This custom-tuned setup with Genie Tech ensures you get consistent, confidence-inspiring suspension performance whether you're charging descents or climbing chunky singletrack.

## Which Levo 4 Should You Buy?

- **S-Works Levo 4**: For riders who want top-tier power (111Nm / 720W), wireless shifting, carbon wheels, and the best suspension spec money can buy.
- **Pro**: A high-performance e-MTB with premium carbon components and excellent trail capability without hitting flagship pricing.
- **Expert**: Balanced build with strong trail performance, ideal for riders who want an advanced spec without going all-in.
- **Comp**: The most affordable Levo 4, great for getting into the full-power e-MTB world with solid hardware and the same range as higher-end models.

## Summary: Is the Turbo Levo 4 Worth It?

The 2025 Turbo Levo 4 lineup delivers power, performance, and customization in four distinct flavors. Whether you're after all-day range, motor-driven speed, or simply want to ride more trails in less time, there's a Levo 4 built for you.

If you're trying to get the most out of shorter rides or want to extend your trail time without burning out, the Levo 4 delivers.
