## Key Takeaways

### Best Value Drivetrain

The **Shimano SLX M7100** is the standout for riders who want modern 12-speed shifting, solid weight savings, and reliable performance—all at a relatively low cost.

### New Budget Options from SRAM

The **SRAM Eagle 70** and **Eagle 90** are strong new entries in the mechanical drivetrain space.

- Eagle 70 is slightly heavier than SX but brings updated tech and improved shifting for a modest price increase.
- Eagle 90 lands closer to GX in performance and weight, making it an attractive mid-tier choice for trail and XC builds.

### Premium Performance

For those focused on lightweight builds or high-performance riding:

- **SRAM X01 Eagle** offers a refined feel with a more aggressive gear range and lower weight than GX or Eagle 90.
- **Shimano XTR M9100** is the lightest option, but also the most expensive, making it best suited for serious racers or riders chasing every performance edge.
